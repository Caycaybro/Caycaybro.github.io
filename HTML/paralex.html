Why Starry Night ???


So...
The story goes... 🧐
Back then...
There was a person's child... She told me a story, about how she once witnessed a gathering of stars that were very numerous and extremely beautiful (just like her, hia hia hia hia) and she also said that it was the most beautiful collection of stars 😯. She had seen others, but the one that stood out the most in terms of quantity and beauty was the one she witnessed at that moment, according to her ~~

Then... at that moment, I tried to imagine the sight of those numerous stars that she mentioned 🤔. Because of that too, it gave me the idea to give this website a starry night theme.(by the way in case you forgot about it, the stars and rockets and the writing move hand in hand as you scroll the screen) Hope you like it 🙂.
